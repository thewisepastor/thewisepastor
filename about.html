About The Rev. Dr. Brian Wise

The Rev. Dr. Brian Wise is a pastor, writer, and consultant dedicated to helping churches and nonprofits craft impactful messages. With a background in pastoral leadership, interfaith dialogue, and nonprofit strategy, Brian brings a unique perspective to faith-based storytelling.

He holds a Bachelor of Arts in Theology from Valparaiso University with a minor in Music, a Master of Divinity from the Lutheran School of Theology at Chicago, and a Doctor of Ministry from the Pacific School of Religion, focusing on pluralism and new religious movements.

Faith & Leadership

As an ordained ELCA pastor, Brian has served in various leadership roles, including his current position as Senior Pastor at Our Saviour’s Lutheran Church in Naperville, IL. His ministry centers around reconciliation, justice, and innovative approaches to faith communication.

Beyond the Pulpit

In addition to his pastoral work, Brian is a sought-after writer and speaker, helping organizations navigate challenges with strategic messaging and creative communication solutions.

Personal Life

Brian is married to Mandy, an elementary school music teacher, and together they have three children—Wesley, Gia, and Wyatt. He’s a lifelong White Sox fan and enjoys spending time with his family and their dog, Pani Paczki.
