Meet Brian Wise

The Rev. Dr. Brian Wise is an ELCA pastor, writer, and consultant specializing in faith-based communication, nonprofit leadership, and spiritual storytelling. With a deep passion for guiding churches and organizations through meaningful conversations, he provides insight and expertise to help leaders craft messages that inspire and heal.

Brian earned his B.A. in Theology from Valparaiso University with a minor in Music and later received his Master of Divinity from the Lutheran School of Theology at Chicago. He recently completed his Doctor of Ministry at the Pacific School of Religion in Berkeley, CA, focusing on pluralism and new religious movements.

With extensive experience serving in both local congregations and broader denominational leadership, Brian understands the complexities that faith communities and nonprofits face today. His ministry has always been rooted in relationships, reconciliation, and justice, helping communities respond to division with love and creativity.

More Than Just a Pastor

Beyond his role in church leadership, Brian is a writer, speaker, and advocate for inclusive faith communities. His work supports churches and organizations seeking to navigate cultural shifts, address tensions, and create welcoming spaces for all people.
