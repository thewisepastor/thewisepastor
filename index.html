About Me

I am an ELCA pastor based in Naperville, passionate about helping churches, nonprofits, and leaders craft impactful messages. With advanced degrees in theology and ministry, I bring a deep understanding of faith-based communication. My experience in interfaith dialogue, nonprofit leadership, and community engagement allows me to write with clarity, authenticity, and purpose.

Understanding the Challenges

We live in a world filled with division, tension, and uncertainty. Churches and nonprofits are at the forefront of navigating these challenges, seeking to offer love, hope, and unity in complex times. I understand that responding with grace and creativity can sometimes require an outside voice. I am here to walk with you—to help craft messages that speak to the heart, offer healing, and inspire action.

Services

  • Creative support for sermon series development
  • Speechwriting for pastors, nonprofit leaders, and executives
  • Faith-based content creation (articles, newsletters, social media)
  • Helping organizations develop strong grant-writing language
  • Ghostwriting for short-term projects and books (with a preference for leadership and faith-based topics)
  • Flexible support for nonprofits and churches, tailored to their specific needs
